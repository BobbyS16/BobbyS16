Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

1. Introduction

PaceRank ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our application available at pacerank.app.

2. Data Controller

Philippe Sallenave, founder of PaceRank
Email: philippe.sallenave@pacerank.app

3. Data We Collect

When you create a PaceRank account, we collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, profile photo, age, city (via Google OAuth or manual entry).
  • Sports activity data: activities you log manually or import from connected services (Strava, Garmin Connect), including distance, duration, pace, discipline, and date.
  • Social data: friend connections within the app.
  • Technical data: log data, IP address, browser type, used for security and debugging purposes.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide the core service: rankings, leaderboards, performance points calculation.
  • Allow you to compare and connect with friends.
  • Authenticate you securely (via Google OAuth).
  • Improve the app and fix bugs.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

PaceRank uses the following trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication (data stored in the EU).
  • Vercel — application hosting.
  • Google OAuth — sign-in service.
  • Strava API (optional) — to import your activities, with your explicit consent.
  • Garmin Connect API (optional, pending approval) — to import your activities, with your explicit consent.

When you connect a third-party service (Strava, Garmin), we only request read-only access to your activity data. You can revoke this access at any time from the third-party service's settings or by contacting us.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers located in the European Union. We apply industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), Row-Level Security (RLS) policies on the database, and authenticated API access.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are based in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for third-party integrations at any time.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: philippe.sallenave@pacerank.app.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain certain information.

9. Cookies

PaceRank uses only essential cookies needed for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at pacerank.app/privacy. Significant changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification.

11. Contact

For any question regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, contact:
